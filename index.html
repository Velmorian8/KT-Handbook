Section 1: Overview

Welcome to the official Handbook of the Knights Templar. This comprehensive guide is designed to provide essential information and guidance to the esteemed members of the Order within the Robloxian Church games. As moderators, Templar members must be well informed on the inner workings of the Order and the Church.

Here, we lay the foundation for a harmonious, organized, and informed Order. The following sections cover vital aspects of life within the community, ensuring all members perform their duties effectively and maintain discipline.

Section 2: Rules of the Order

Rule 1: Responsibility, Respect, and Conduct

  • All members are accountable for their actions, including addressing issues like corruption, trolling, and other offences.
  • Discrimination against fellow members is strictly prohibited.
  • Templar Knights are expected to maintain high standards of respect within the community.
  • All members are required to adhere to the specified uniform guidelines.

Rule 2: Abuse of Power

  • Any granted power must not be misused; Admin commands are to be used only when necessary.
  • Knights and higher-ranking members must refrain from belittling fellow members.

Rule 3: Activity

  • Members must stay active and meet their Activity Point Quota to remain in the Order.
  • Members unable to meet their Quota or requiring significant leave may request an Inactivity Notice, providing the necessary information.

Rule 4: Discharging

  • Members may discharge with at least a 5+ day service to the Order, starting from their joining date.
  • Members who desert the Order without submitting a valid discharge request will face punishments depending on their rank & standing.

Section 4: Chain of Command

Low-Ranks (Templar Sergeants)

The entry point into the Order. Limited authority and recognition. Progress through active participation, handling infractions, and commitment.

High-Ranks (Templar Knights)

Competent leaders who host events, manage low ranks, and ensure smooth operations. Can advance to High Command and earn medals.

HICOM Ranks (Templar High-Command)

Most prestigious and powerful members. Lead from the helm, oversee events, select high ranks, and assist the College of Cardinals.

Section 6: Promotions

Members of the Knights Templar are promoted through Activity Points (AP), earned by attending events, logging moderator actions, and interacting with the community.

Event/Task AP Gain

  • RC Sunday Mass: 60 AP
  • RC Weekday Mass: 30 AP
  • RC Major Public Events: 40 AP
  • Ordinations: 200 AP
  • Templar Inspections: 150 AP
  • Templar Exclusive Events: 40 AP (Trainings/Lectures only, max 3 per week)
  • Altar Servers Club: 30 AP
  • Logging Moderator Work: 5 AP per troll removed (Kickwarn/ban), 2 AP per warning(s), 5 AP per hour of playtime — max 60 AP per day for these combined

Initiate: Starting rank

Man-At-Arms: 200 AP

Militia: 300 AP

Footman: 400 AP

Veteran Footman / Scout: 500 AP

Squire: 600 AP

Knight: Knighthood application

Elite Knight: 700 AP

Lieutenant: 800 AP

Captain: 1000 AP

Commander: 1200 AP

Major: 1400 AP

Major General: 1600 AP

Explanation: After reaching a certain AP, you get promoted accordingly. Max 3 promotions for Low-Rank, 2 for High-Rank. Quota is monthly (30 days) and revised on the 1st.

  • 200 AP required monthly for Low-Rank (3+ events).
  • 350 AP required monthly for High-Rank (5+ events).
  • Quota can be completed via moderation logs or event logs.
