Section 6: Promotions

Members are promoted through Activity Points (AP) earned by attending events, logging moderation actions, and community interaction.

Event/Task AP Gain

  • RC Sunday Mass: 60 AP
  • RC Weekday Mass: 30 AP
  • RC Major Public Events: 40 AP
  • Ordinations: 200 AP
  • Templar Inspections: 150 AP
  • Templar Exclusive Events (3/week max): 40 AP
  • Altar Servers Club: 30 AP
  • Logging Moderator Work: 5 AP per troll removed, 2 AP per warning, 5 AP per hour of playtime (60 AP daily cap)
