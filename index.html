Section 1: Overview

Welcome to the official Handbook of the Knights Templar. This guide provides essential knowledge for all members within the Robloxian Church games. Moderators and Templar members must understand the inner workings of the Order to maintain harmony and organization.

The following sections cover rules, uniforms, chain of command, formations, and promotions to help all members perform their duties with excellence.

Section 2: Rules of the Order

Rule 1: Responsibility, Respect, and Conduct

  • All members are accountable for their actions.
  • Discrimination is strictly prohibited.
  • High standard of respect expected from Templar Knights.
  • Uniforms must be followed.

Rule 2: Abuse of Power

  • Admin powers must be used only when necessary.
  • Knights must not belittle other members.

Rule 3: Activity

  • Members must meet their Activity Point quota.
  • Requests for inactivity require proper notice.

Rule 4: Discharging

  • Discharge requires minimum 5+ days of service.
  • Desertion without notice will lead to punishment based on rank.

Section 4: Chain of Command

  • Low-Ranks (Templar Sergeants): Entry-level, limited authority, progress via events and commitment.
  • High-Ranks (Templar Knights): Host events, manage low-ranks, advance to High Command.
  • HICOM Ranks (Templar High-Command): Lead, oversee events, select high-ranks, assist College of Cardinals.

Section 6: Promotions

Promotion is earned through Activity Points (AP) by attending events, logging moderation actions, and community participation.

Templar Sergeant Personnel
  • Initiate: Starting rank
  • Man-At-Arms: 200 AP
  • Militia: 300 AP
  • Footman: 400 AP
  • Veteran Footman: 500 AP
  • Scout: 500 AP
  • Squire: 600 AP
Knighthood Personnel
  • Knight: Knighthood application
  • Elite Knight: 700 AP
  • Lieutenant: 800 AP
  • Captain: 1000 AP
  • Commander: 1200 AP
  • Major: 1400 AP
  • Major General: 1600 AP
